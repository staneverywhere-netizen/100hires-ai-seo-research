# Lily Ray

## Video

The SEO Mistake Destroying Your Website Rankings

## Focus

AI-generated content, search quality, E-E-A-T, and Google's evolving ranking systems.

## Summary

Lily explains that many websites fail because they prioritize scale over expertise. She argues that AI content can be useful when supported by real expertise, but becomes risky when used to mass-produce low-quality pages.

## Key Points

- Human-first content performs better long term.

- E-E-A-T is a major differentiator.

- Original experience is difficult for AI to replicate.

- Building trust is more important than exploiting shortcuts.

- Sustainable SEO outperforms short-term tactics.

## Notes

A recurring theme in Lily's work is that authentic expertise and trust signals are becoming increasingly important as AI-generated content becomes more common.
