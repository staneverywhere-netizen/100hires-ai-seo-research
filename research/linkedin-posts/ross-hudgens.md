# Ross Hudgens

## Profile

Founder and CEO of Siege Media, a content marketing and SEO agency known for scaling organic traffic through high-quality content.

## Why Included

Ross Hudgens has extensive experience in content production, content marketing, link building, and SEO growth strategies. He frequently discusses how AI can accelerate content workflows while maintaining quality standards.

## Recent Content Themes

### AI-Assisted Content Production

Ross emphasizes that AI can help teams create content faster, but successful companies still invest heavily in editing, design, and expert review.

### Content Quality Over Quantity

He argues that publishing fewer high-quality articles often produces better results than publishing large volumes of low-quality content.

### Topical Authority

Ross frequently discusses building comprehensive content hubs around important business topics rather than targeting isolated keywords.

### SEO and Content Strategy

He recommends aligning content creation with business goals rather than chasing search volume alone.

## Key Takeaways

- AI improves efficiency but not expertise.
- Human editing remains essential.
- High-quality content outperforms mass-produced content.
- Topical authority strengthens organic visibility.
- Content should support business objectives, not just rankings.
