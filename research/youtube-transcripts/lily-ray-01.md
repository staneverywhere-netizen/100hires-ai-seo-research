# Lily Ray

## Video

The Future of SEO in the Age of AI

## Summary

Lily Ray discusses how AI is changing search and content production. She explains that businesses should focus on expertise, trust, and original insights rather than relying entirely on AI-generated content.

## Key Points

- AI is a useful assistant, not a replacement for experts.
- E-E-A-T continues to influence rankings.
- Original research performs better than generic content.
- Brand authority is becoming increasingly important.

## Notes

Successful AI SEO strategies combine automation with human expertise and quality control.
