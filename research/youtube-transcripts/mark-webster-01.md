# Mark Webster

## Video

Scaling SEO Content Production with AI

## Summary

Mark Webster explains how content teams can use AI to increase production speed while maintaining quality through structured workflows and editorial review.

## Key Points

- AI assists with research and drafting.
- Human editors improve quality and accuracy.
- Search intent should drive content decisions.
- Content systems are more important than individual tactics.
- Long-term success comes from consistency.

## Notes

Mark's approach focuses on combining AI efficiency with proven SEO processes and quality assurance.
