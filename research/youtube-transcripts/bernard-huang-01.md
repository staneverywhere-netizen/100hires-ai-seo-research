# Bernard Huang

## Video

Using AI to Improve SEO Content Quality

## Summary

Bernard Huang explains how AI tools can assist with content research, optimization, and workflow efficiency. He emphasizes that successful SEO content must still provide value, expertise, and relevance to users.

## Key Points

- AI helps streamline content production.
- Search intent remains critical.
- Content quality drives long-term performance.
- Optimization should focus on user needs.
- Human expertise improves AI-generated content.

## Notes

Bernard's approach combines AI-assisted workflows with strong editorial standards and content optimization best practices.
