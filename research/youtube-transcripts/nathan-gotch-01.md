## Video

How to Use AI for SEO Content Production

## Summary

Nathan Gotch explains how AI tools can help SEO teams accelerate content creation without sacrificing quality. He recommends combining AI-generated drafts with expert editing and optimization.

## Key Points

- AI speeds up content production.
- Human review improves trust and accuracy.
- Keyword research remains essential.
- Content should be optimized for search intent.
- Regular content updates improve performance.

## Notes

Nathan's approach focuses on building efficient SEO systems that combine automation with expert oversight.
