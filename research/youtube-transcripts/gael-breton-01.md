# Gael Breton

## Video

AI SEO: Building Content Systems That Scale

## Summary

Gael Breton explains how AI can improve content production efficiency while maintaining quality through structured editorial processes.

## Key Points

- AI helps with research and outlines.
- Human review remains critical.
- Topical authority improves ranking potential.
- Content systems should be repeatable and scalable.
- SEO success requires consistency and quality.

## Notes

Gael advocates combining AI tools with strong editorial standards rather than relying entirely on automation.
