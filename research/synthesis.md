# Research Findings

## Finding 1: AI Is Primarily Used for Drafting

Most experts use AI for:

- Keyword research
- Topic clustering
- Content briefs
- First drafts

## Finding 2: Human Editing Remains Critical

Experts consistently recommend reviewing AI-generated content before publication.

## Finding 3: Topical Authority Matters

Publishing many articles is less important than covering a topic comprehensively.

## Finding 4: Successful AI SEO Workflows

Most successful workflows combine:

- AI tools
- Human expertise
- Content strategy
- Internal linking
